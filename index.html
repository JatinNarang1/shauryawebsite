Our Products

White Masterbatches

One of our flagship offerings includes our range of White Msterbatches, available with loading percentages ranging from 18% to 75%. These masterbatches are meticulously formulated to provide super opacity, brightness, and color consistency, making them ideal for applications across industries such as packaging, textiles and consumer goods.

Black Masterbatches

Our range of black masterbatches stands out with unparalleled jetness and a carbon loading capacity of up to 55%. Crafted with precision, they offer superior color intensity and consistency, making them ideal for diverse applications across industries like plastics, packaging, automotive, and more

Colour Masterbatches

At Panchsheel Trading Company, we offer a diverse spectrum of meticulously crafted color masterbatches to meet client needs. From vibrant hues to subtle tones, our range caters to various industries like packaging, textiles, automotive, and more. What sets us apart is our customization ability, tailoring formulations to exact specifications, bringing visions to life, and differentiating products in the market

Filler Masterbatches

Our lineup features filler grades, notably calcium carbonate, prized for high opacity and performance. Ideal for opacity-sensitive applications, they enhance both aesthetics and functionality. Additionally, we provide talc-based and sodium-based fillers, tailored for excellent transparency in LDPE/LLDPE films, ensuring unmatched clarity and consistency, particularly crucial for transparent film applications.

Additives

Our additive masterbatches include: • Slip Agent: Reduces friction, enhancing plastic surface slip. • Antistatic: Effectively mitigates static charges • Polymer Processing Aids: Enhances melt flow and dispersion efficiency. • VCI: Provides corrosion protection for metal components packaged with plastics. • Optical Brighteners: Elevates plastic products' whiteness and brightness. • Biodegradable Plastic: Facilitates eco-friendly plastic product development. • Oleamide and Erucamide Waxes: Enhances plastic material performance and aesthetics.

Compostable Material

• PBAT: A flexible and durable thermoplastic polyester, offering biodegradability without compromising functionality. • PLA: Derived from renewable sources, fully compostable with properties akin to traditional plastics, suitable for diverse applications. • Bio-fillers: Formulated to boost biodegradability and compostability, made from natural materials to reduce environmental impact.
